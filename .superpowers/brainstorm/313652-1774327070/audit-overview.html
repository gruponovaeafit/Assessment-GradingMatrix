Priority Areas

Select the areas that are most critical for initial implementation:

A

Authentication Security

Log all login attempts (success/fail), logouts, and token revocations. Focus on identifying potential brute-force or unauthorized access.

B

Administrative Actions

Log creation, updates, and deletion of Staff members. Know exactly which admin created which account.

C

Assessment Integrity

Log creation and deletion of Assessments and Groups. Critical for preventing accidental data loss.

D

Grading Operations

Log when grades are submitted or modified. Useful for resolving disputes about scores.
